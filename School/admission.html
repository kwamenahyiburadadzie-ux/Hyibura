Admissions at Sunrise Academy

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Previous school records/transcripts
  • Proof of age (birth certificate/passport)
  • The need of Health Insurance(NHIS Number)
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Interview with student and parent/guardian

How to Apply

We welcome applications throughout the academic year. Please fill out the form below to submit an inquiry or begin your application process. Our admissions team will contact you for the next steps.
